The Dread Imperium

Remnants of the Imperium


Turmoil has engulfed the Dread Imperium. The remnants have left and set of on Star Destroyers to make a new life. They are all that is left and are looking for a new home and the opportunity to rebuild.

Now calling themselves The Remnants of the Imperium, their once pristine and orderly ships are now covered in laundry, looking like the refugee camps they are.
